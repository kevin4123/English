Reference: K-12

grade

Meaning: (SCHOOL YEAR) one of the 12 years that students are at school in the American school system, or the students in a particular year

Example: My brother is in sixth grade.

grade

Meaning: (MARK IN SCHOOL) a mark that a student is given for their work or for an examination

Example: He got a grade A in maths.

grade

Meaning: to give a mark to an examination paper or to a piece of school work

Example: Ted is grading papers in his office.
