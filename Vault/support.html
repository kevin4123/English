support

Meaning: (HOLDING SOMETHING UP) something that presses on something else to hold it up or in position

Example: the wooden supports of the bridge

support

Meaning: (FOR PART OF BODY) something that presses on something else to hold it up or in position

Example: neck support

support

Meaning: (HOLD SOMETHING UP) to hold the weight of something, keep it in place, or prevent it from falling

Example: The middle part of the bridge is supported by two huge towers.

support

Meaning: (AGREE AND HELP) to say that you agree with an idea, group, or person, and usually to help them because you want them to succeed

Example: We need to support our teachers in their efforts to raise standards.
