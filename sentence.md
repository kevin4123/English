## When you don't understand a word
What does [word] mean?
Can you explain the word [word] in simple English?
How do I use the word [word] in a sentence?

## When you want to check your grammar
Is this sentence correct: [your sentence]?
How can I say [your sentence] more naturally?
Can you fix the grammar in this: [your sentence]?

## When you want to know the difference between two things
What is the difference between [Word A] and [Word B]?
When should I use [Word A] instead of [Word B]?

## When you know the idea but not the word
How do you say [describe the thing] in English?
What is the English word for [describe the action]?
